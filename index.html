Technical Courses We Offer

Explore the intersection of technology and society. By providing technical skills training for your employees, you're instilling self-confidence that they have the knowledge and competence to perform their daily tasks to the best of their ability.

Software Engineering

Software engineering is a modern course focused on the development of algorithmic and software. Students acquire mathematical knowledge, study modern programming languages, software testing, operating systems, databases and other disciplines.

System Analysis

System analysis is a scientific method of cognition, which is a sequence of actions to establish structural relationships between variables or elements of the studied system. It is based on a complex of general scientific, statistical, mathematical methods.

Information Systems

The main goal of the Information Systems course is the development and administration of complex IT infrastructure solutions using Data Science and AI technologies. Much time is devoted to studying the architecture of cloud information systems using ML and AI technologies.

Our Facilities

which combines educational, scientific and innovative potentials.

World Class Library

The library, together with the KPI community and for the KPI community, develops a creative, comfortable and safe environment that promotes research, learning and teaching, and self-development, with the goal of building a world-class research university

Big Sports Complex

The KPI sports complex is a place where students spend their free time. One of the advantages of the sports complex is the diversity of the selection of sections, which is quite liked and attracts students. Everyone chooses the sport that they like the most.

Tasty and Healthy Food

The cafeteria performs functions related to the nutrition of students and employees of the university. Confectionery and culinary products, drinks, juices, dairy and other products are baked in the catering center.
